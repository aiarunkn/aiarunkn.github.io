My story.

I grew up performing. Dance, theatre, stage competitions across Astana. You stop caring about people's opinions pretty fast when you're under the lights — and you learn that the take you give is the take they keep. So you land it the first time.

At some point I started building instead. No course, no bootcamp. I wanted something to exist, so I figured out how to make it exist.

In 2024, I built Kazakhstan's first freight marketplace. My uncle had a truck and couldn't find loads. I walked up to shippers, collected contacts, connected the market that was already there. Solo. Two weeks. Live with users at launch.

Now I'm in San Francisco. Sophomore at Minerva University, double-majoring in Business and Computer Science. Still building.
