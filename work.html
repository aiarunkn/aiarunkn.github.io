01

i built Kazakhstan's first freight marketplace. solo.

my uncle is a carrier with a truck and couldn't find loads. so in Astana I went physical. i walked up to shippers in fairs, found their emails, collected their contact cards. turned out they were all looking for carriers too. the market was there, nobody had connected it. i bootstrapped it and shipped the first version in two weeks, with already 2 shippers on the app at launch. i faked 3–4 postings at first to build trust in them. for now, 1 load is delivered safely.

(initially i got yelled at by a logistician with a national award (27+ years in logistics) because i came in to his office with a barely working mvp lol)

14days, idea → live with users
