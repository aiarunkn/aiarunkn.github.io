01 The proof

I built Kazakhstan's first freight marketplace. Solo.

My uncle is a carrier with a truck and couldn't find loads. So in Astana I went physical — walked up to shippers, found their emails, collected contact cards. Turned out they were all looking for carriers too. The market was there; nobody had connected it.

I bootstrapped it and shipped the first version in two weeks, with real users on the app at launch.
